SmartSave Website Plan

Site Name

SmartSave

The name "SmartSave" reflects the goal of providing smart, beginner-friendly guidance on saving and growing money. It is memorable, easy to spell, and relevant to the siteâ€™s purpose of financial literacy and saving.

Site Purpose

The website will educate young adults on how to save and grow their money effectively through clear explanations of compound interest, saving vs. investing, and setting personal financial goals. It will offer interactive tools such as an investment calculator and dynamic financial tips, all within a responsive and accessible design.

Scenarios

  • How can I understand the benefits of compound interest to grow my savings over time?
  • What practical steps can I take to set and achieve my personal saving goals?

Color Schema

Primary Color (#1B4F9C): Used for headings, navigation links, and buttons to provide a professional and trustworthy feel.

Accent Color (#208B6D): Used for icons, callouts, and highlights to draw attention without overwhelming the user.

Background Color (#F9F9F9): Used as the main page background to keep the site light and readable.

Typography

  • Roboto, sans-serif: Used for body text and paragraphs for clean readability.
  • Montserrat, sans-serif: Used for headings (H1, H2, H3).

Fallback fonts include Arial and Helvetica for compatibility.
